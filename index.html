CONTRACT FARMING is an agreement between farmers and Contractors( marketing firms ) for the production and supply of agricultural products under forward agreements

Welcome Farmers

For Farmers Contract Farming reduces the risk of production, price and marketing costs. Contract farming can open up new markets which would otherwise be unavailable to small farmers. It also ensures higher production of better quality, financial support in cash or kind and technical guidance to the farmer.

Welcome contractrs

Contractors can enter into a direct contract with farmers will ensure better control on the quality of the end-product with both stakeholders having an equal interest in its success. Directly contracting with buyers will increase competition leading to higher prices of farm produce for farmers and higher income .
